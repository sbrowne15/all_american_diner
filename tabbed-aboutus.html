About Us

So You Want to Know More?

Located on the east side of Air Depot Blvd near the intersection with Reno Ave in Midwest City, OK, we have been serving up standard diner fare and local Favorites since 2012. We serve our entire menu all day long, and have daily specials that keep things interesting. Whether you are an early riser that needs a breakfast that will make you ready to take on the day or want a nice home-cooked dinner after a long day at work, we've got you covered. We even have pies and other homemade desserts for anyone that has a sweet-tooth that fires off after dinner. If you can't stay, call ahead and pick it up to take out.

New in 2020, we've partnered with DoorDash to offer delivery across the Mid-Del area and east to Choctaw.
